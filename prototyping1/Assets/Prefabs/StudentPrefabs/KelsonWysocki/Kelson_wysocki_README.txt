Player launch:

Public variables:
Damage Area: should just be set to the DamageArea.prefab in my folder.
Launch distance: how far the dash goes. adjusts speed of the dash to make time to dash stay the same.
Cooldown: how long it takes the dash to recharge after being used.

Keys: 
"Left Click"
	click: Dashes default distance with small damage area.
	hold : Hold to charge dash. It takes 0.5 seconds before increase in distance/damage area size starts. Once started it takes 1 second to fully charge.

Instructions:
Add "PlayerLaunchPrefab" to level.
