The monsterEye enemy prefab (hereafter called "the eye") is meant to be placed anywhere in the level. The eye follows the player and damages them when the player comes into contact with its beam. This is meant to model the player getting hurt when they're seen.
Speed - how fast it moves
Hunger - how strong the vector to move toward the player is. meant to be between 0 and 1.
Range - the distance from which the eye can harm the player
Damage - how much damage the player takes per tick when seen
